    • Character clips 2

    In this section we will look at some of the pinnacle moments for our characters throughout the show. These are just some of my favourites.




    "Church scene"

    In this clip we see Jackie Moreno, a broken down studio producer, noticing Mylene, as she sings her break out solo against her fathers will. Throughout the clip you can see her father flabbergasted by the scene.

    Telepathy

    Telepathy is a song that is performed by Christina Aguilera and used in the show to help set a scene where Dizzee explores his sexuality with one of his fellow graffiti artists at a secret underground party.


    Flash Featurette

    In this clip we see Grandmaster Flash explaining his role in the show and how he was asked to be a character in The Get Down. Flash teaches some of the actors how to scratch and mix for the show, to give it that authentic look.


    The Final Get Down

    This clips shows The Get Down Brothers in their final showdown against The Notorious 3. Being one of the final scenes in the series it showcases all of the skill the brothers have learned along their journey.
