    In this section we will take a look at the main characters from The Get Down, to give you a better idea of their background and role within the show.




    Shameik Moore - Shaolin 'Shao' Fantastic

    In the show, Shao is the one who has the link to the underground music scene that is The Get Down. His goal is to be trained by Grandmaster Flash (a local legend) and become the greatest DJ worldwide. To achieve this he must first find his wordsmith and train to escape the life of drugs and crime that he has been forced into.

    Justice Smith - Ezekiel "Books" Figuero

    Books is a student on the rise, and has the potential to go to an ivy league school, with his family and teacher pushing him in that direction. Books is in love with Mylene and hopes to escape the Bronx with her, but little does he know that he is destined to become Shaoâ€™s wordsmith and be fast tracked to stardom through the music that they will make together.


    Herizen Guardiola - Mylene Cruz

    Mylene is the niece of Papa Fuerte, and the daughter of the leaders of the local Pentecostal church. Her father allows her to sing during Sunday church, but forbids her to follow her dream of becoming a world famous disco singer and dancer. Books and her uncle support her dream and try to assist her in achieving it.


    Jaden Smith - Marcus 'Dizzee' Kipling

    The eldest of the Kipling children, Dizzee is vastly different than his brothers and sister. Dizzee prefers to express himself through art, particularly graffiti, and loves to live life to the full, even it if means a little danger every now and then. Dizzee is known around the Bronx as one of the finest graffiti artists New York has to offer.
