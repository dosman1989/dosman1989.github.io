    In this section we will take a look at the main characters from The Get Down, to give you a better idea of their background and role within the show. COntinued from page one.




    Jimmy Smits - Francisco 'Papa Fuerte' Cruz

    Papa Fuertes is a hustler who uses his influence in the community to edge his way into politics in a corrupt New York City. He would use his power to draw attention to the struggling south Bronx streets, via forging agreements with the mayor of New York. Papa would see Mylene succeed and become a disco legend through his contacts in the music industry.

    Skylan Brooks - Ronald 'Ra-Ra' Kipling

    Ra-Ra is the middle son of the Kipling family, and one of the main members of The Get Down Brothers (A group put together by Books and Shao). As the one with his head screwed on, Ra-Ra is both a loyal and protective friend.


    Tremaine Brown Jr. - Mile 'Boo Boo' Kipling

    The youngest of the Kipling family, Boo Boo is that brother that always wants to go with his older brother, and do what he does. Full of curiosity and enery, Boo is the youngest member of The Get Down brothers.


    Mamoudou Athie - Grandmaster Flash

    Grandmaster Flash is known in the Hip Hop community as a legend and pioneer, teaching many of the greatest artists their craft, he is revered the world over. In the show, Flash takes Shao under his wing to teach him the ways of “the get down.”
